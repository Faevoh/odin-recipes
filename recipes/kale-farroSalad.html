Kale and Farro Salad

Description

The Kale and Farro Salad is the perfect addition to your meal and perfect when craving a quick lunch

It is a packed combination of earthy farro, nutrient-dense kale and crunchy walnuts that creates a dishe that satisfies both your appetite and your wellness goals. Packed with fibre, antioxidnts and healthy fats, making it ideal for a refeshing burst of flavours.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Extra-Virgin Olive oil
  • 1/3 cup of Lemon Juice
  • 3 Garlic Clove (finely minced)
  • 3 Tbsp of Tahini
  • 1/4 tsp of fine Sea Salt
  • 1/4 tsp of Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • 6 cups of Kale (stems removed and finely chopped)
  • 1 cup of Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 cup of Chopped Walnuts
  • 1 1/2 cups of Dry Farro

Steps

  1. Prepare the farro:
    • Bring a large pot of water to boil
    • Add farro and reduce heat to medium-high and cook uncovered until soft, about 30 minutes.
    • Strain
  2. Make the lemon tahini dressing:
    • Add olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, tahini, salt and pepper to a small bowl
    • Whisk to combine
    • Set aside
  3. Assemble the salad:
    • Add kale, cooked farro, Parmesan cheese and walnuts to a large bowl
    • Add dressing and toss to coat
  4. Serve, topped with additional Parmesan cheeseif desired. Keep in the fridge in an airtight container for 2-3 days
  5. Substitutions:
    • Substitute farro for grain of your choice. Bular or quinoa would work well
    • Swap kale for leafy green of your choice, although skicking with a hearty green such as collard greens are recommended
