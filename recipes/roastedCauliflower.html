Whole Roasted Cauliflower with Dressing

Description

The Whole roasteed cauliflower is a stunning centerpiece and a nutritional powerhouse, perfect for supporting digestion and hormonal balance.

The creamy tahini dressing adds a rich, nutty flavour, while the bright notes of parsley and lemon juice brings the meal to life.

Ingredients

  • 1/8 cup of Extra-Virgin Olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp of Lemon Juice
  • 1 Garlic Clove (small, roughly chopped)
  • 1/4 cup of Tahini
  • 3 Tbsp of Avocado Oil
  • 3/4 tsp of Himalayan Pink Salt
  • 1 tsp of Maple Syrup
  • 1/2 cup of Filtered Water
  • 1/3 cup of Fresh Parsley (finely chopped)

Steps

  1. Preheat:
    • Oven to 425F (degree Fahrenheit)
    • Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Boil:
    • A large pot of water, enought to cover at least half of the cauliflower head
    • Place the whole cauliflower head in the pot and cover, cooking for about 10 minutes, flipping the cauliflower halfway through
    • Cook unitl the cauliflower is tender.
  3. Dry:
    • The cauliflowerby patting with a towel
    • Place the cauliflower on the baking sheet and rub avocado oil all over it.
    • Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp of salt
    • Roast the cauliflower for 20-30 minutes, until it is browned and somewhat crispy on the outside
  4. Blend:
    • The lemon juice, 1/4 tsp of salt, tahini, olive oil, garlic and maple syrup.
    • With the blender running, slowly add the water until the consistency of the tahini dressing is smooth and somewhat runny.
  5. Drizzle the dressing on the cauliflower and sprinkle the parsley over the top
  6. To serve:
    • Slice cauliflower into wedges or lengthwise into steaks
    • Drizzle the additional tahini dressing and parsley
